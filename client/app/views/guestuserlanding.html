WeFeast

Welcome

Going out to eat with a group and having trouble picking a restaurant that appeals to everyone? WeFeast is a restaurant recommendation platform where users ("hosts") can invite friends, family, and colleagues to join them for a meal. Each invitee is emailed an invitation and may come to WeFeast to vote for the type of food they prefer. Once everyone has voted, our recommendation engine will provide the host a selection of restaurants chosen to best satisfy everyone on the guest list.
